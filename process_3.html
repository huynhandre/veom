Generate Structure

The limit value is a parameter, one can change, when the geometry should grow together. The voxels, not influenced by the attractor impact and the limit, are deleted. This decision is made by a "remap function", which gives all the voxels a value in terms of their distance to the attractor and the limit. If the value is big enough, a geometry is displayed.
